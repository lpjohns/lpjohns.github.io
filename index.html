About me

My midwest childhood

I grew up on the East side of wonderful Madison, Wisconsin with my inspiring and creative parents and sister.

The travel bug

In 2001 I travelled to Israel and then studied in Belgium. Through these two trips I caught the travel bug and have lived in the US, UK and Middle East since then.

Career then, now and in the future

My professional career started in HR consulting and continued in strategy consulting. On the side I also started a jewellery business, and am always bursting with ideas for new ventures. Watch this space.

My other loves

When I'm not working, I love making jewellery, discovering new music, exploring cities with my awesome partner-in-crime and husband, running, making gluten-free foods and trying my hand at various different crafts.
